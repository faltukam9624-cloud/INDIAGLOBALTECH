FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does it understand Hindi and other languages, or just translate word-for-word?

+
IndiaGlobalTech uses advanced contextual AI that understands idioms, intent, cultural nuance, and tone — not just vocabulary. When you write "bhai payment atak gaya hai, client ko thoda pressure dalo professional tarike se," the AI understands you need a firm but professional payment follow-up. The output reads as if written by a native English-speaking professional with 15+ years of experience.

Is my data stored or used to train AI models?

+
No. Your inputs and outputs are never stored permanently or used to train any AI model. Each session is processed in real-time and immediately discarded. We are SOC2 compliant and GDPR ready. Business customers receive a Data Processing Agreement upon request.

How is this different from ChatGPT or Google Translate?

+
Google Translate converts words without understanding professional context. ChatGPT is general-purpose and requires skilled prompting for consistent results. IndiaGlobalTech is purpose-built for professional business communication with pre-optimized output formats, industry-specific tone calibration, and specialized tools like ResumeForge and PitchDeck AI with no equivalent in generic AI platforms.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

+
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your Pro or Business subscription anytime from your account dashboard. There are no cancellation fees, no lock-in periods, and no questions asked. Your plan stays active until the end of your current billing period, then automatically reverts to the free plan.

Does the team plan support multiple users?

+
Our Business plan supports up to 10 seats with centralized billing, team analytics, and custom brand voice settings. Every team member gets their own login and usage tracking. For larger organizations (50+ seats), contact us for Enterprise pricing with volume discounts and a dedicated account manager.
