FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does it actually understand Hindi and other languages, or just translate word-for-word?

+
IndiaGlobalTech uses advanced contextual AI that understands idioms, intent, cultural nuance, and conversational tone — not just vocabulary. When you write "bhai payment atak gaya hai, client ko thoda pressure dalo professional tarike se," the AI understands you need a firm but professional payment follow-up, not a literal translation. The output reads as if written by a native English-speaking professional with 15+ years of experience.

Is my data stored or used to train AI models?

+
No. Your inputs and outputs are never stored permanently or used to train any AI model. Each session is processed in real-time and immediately discarded. We are SOC2 compliant and fully GDPR ready. Business plan customers receive a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) upon request.

How is this different from ChatGPT or Google Translate?

+
Google Translate converts words — it does not understand professional context or intent. ChatGPT is a general-purpose assistant that requires skilled prompting to get consistent professional results. IndiaGlobalTech is purpose-built for professional business communication, with pre-optimized output formats, industry-specific tone calibration, and specialized tools like ResumeForge and PitchDeck AI that have no equivalent in generic AI platforms.

Can I connect my own domain — like indiaglobaltech.com — to this platform?

+
Yes, absolutely. If you host this platform on Vercel, connecting your custom domain takes under 2 minutes. In Vercel's dashboard, go to your project → Settings → Domains → Add Domain → type indiaglobaltech.com. Vercel will show you the DNS records to add at your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.). Once you update those records, your site goes live at your domain with full SSL — automatic and free. It typically propagates within 10–30 minutes.

Can teams use this together? Is there a team plan?

+
Our Business plan supports up to 10 seats with centralized billing, team usage analytics, and custom brand voice settings so every team member's output aligns with your company's communication style. For larger organizations (50+ seats), contact us directly for Enterprise pricing with volume discounts and a dedicated account manager.
